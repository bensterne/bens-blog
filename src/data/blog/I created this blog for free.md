Here is the first blog to my personal site. I have built this with Obsidian, Astro, and Cloudflare pages. And it is a completely free setup.

<img src="/ohsnap.gif" width="400" />

For years, I paid a bunch of money to Webflow for my portfolio site, something absolutely critical for someone like myself working in product design. 

That said, I have been in a great role for a few years, and the cost stopped making sense. I have also begun programming on a daily basis, and so found this situation a perfect opportunity to build something to replace Webflow.
